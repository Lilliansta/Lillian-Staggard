My Future

It's a Hobbie?

Ever since I got into Photography I have always wanted to beconme a professional, but as I have gotten older I realise that it might be better as a hobbie or a side gig. Whether it's the thrill of chasing the perfect shot or the joy of sharing one's vision with others, photography remains a beloved hobby that enriches my life and sparks endless curiosity.

I'd like to keep my options open and see what other careers I can do in the media industry.

Other then photography I find videotography intresting too.

Travel?

If I was to become a photograher I'd like to be someone who travels around the world visiting different countries and admiring it's beauty.

As I hardly leave my backyard when taking photos I think it would be nice to get more exposure to different settuings and environments.

Inspiration?

  • My Father and Auntie
  • Min Heejin - Although she is not the greatest person to look up too, her artwork really speaks for its self.
